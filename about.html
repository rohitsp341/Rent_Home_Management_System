Types of Rental Homes:



Apartments: Multi-unit buildings with separate rental units.

Houses: Single-family homes that are available for rent.

Condos: Privately owned units within a larger complex, often with shared amenities.

Townhouses: Multi-level homes that share walls with neighboring units.
